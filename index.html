A quick quide on how to remove all the emails that I send

First, you will need to do these things:

  1. Wait for all the emails to come through.
  2. Make sure you have a long string of words without spaces on your email. If you don't, you're screwed.
  3. Copy the long string of words.
  4. Go to outlook.com, and yes, it has to be the website version.
  5. Click the sign-in button at the top right and log in.
  6. Click the gear icon in the top right.
  7. Click on the link in the bottom that says "View all outlook settings"
  8. Click on rules, which is fourch from the top.
  9. Click 'Add new rule,' then give it a name. This rule is just temporary, so the name doesn't have to be anything fancy.
  10. Set the condidion 'Message body includes' and the action delete.
  11. Scroll to the bottom, and check the box 'Run rule now,' then click save.
  12. Wait for the circle to stop moving and become a triangle.
  13. Delete the rule. Now, all the emails will be deleted!
